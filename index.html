Who is Mathai Fenn?

Mathai Fenn is a cognitive psychologist and educator whose work focuses on how people think, decide, and learn in situations of uncertainty. He completed his postgraduate studies at Kariyavattom and his PhD at IIT Bombay, and went on to teach for nearly a decade at XLRI Jamshedpur. In addition to his work in India, he has taught and conducted academic and professional programs in other countries, including Armenia and Dubai, engaging with diverse student and professional groups. Over the years, he has worked closely with students, families, educators, and caregivers to help them move beyond information overload toward clearer thinking and better questions. His current work explores how AI can be used not as a shortcut to answers, but as a reflective tool that supports sense-making, judgment, and responsible action in everyday life.
