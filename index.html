ASK! Seminar

In-Person & Interactive · Conversations for Clear Thinking

In a nutshell

ASK! is an in-person seminar built around a simple idea: in a world shaped by AI, the most important skill is knowing what to ask — and when.

On 11 January in Trivandrum, ASK! brings together two facilitated sessions that explore this idea in different life contexts — learning and career direction, and navigating health and care decisions.

This is not a lecture or a motivational talk. It is a guided, interactive seminar designed to help people slow down, reflect, and ask better questions before acting.

ASK!!

What is ASK!?

ASK! is an interactive seminar where participants learn by doing — using AI as a thinking aid to clarify questions rather than receive ready-made answers.

What is Mentora?

Mentora is a reflective learning system that helps individuals build a personalised, adaptive career and learning plan using AI.

Primary audience: young adults (18–25). Secondary audience: educators and guidance counsellors.

What is Clariva?

Clariva helps families keep track of a person’s health using a structured, cumulative case file built from medical records and personal inputs.

Clariva enhances — not replaces — the relationship with doctors.
